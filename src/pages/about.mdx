# About us

Welcome to Packrs, a local delivery service platform. We're dedicated to giving you the best services, with a focus on three characteristics, i.e: dependability, customer service, and uniqueness. It all started when our founders decided to make the lives of Indian consumers easier by changing the way we order - all with just a tap!

Founded in 2019 by Perminder Klair and like-minded guys, Packrs aims at transforming the present delivery system by making the delivery of everything possible, not just-food. Currently operating in Jalandhar, Packrs aims at expanding the service to more cities. We have a close-knit team of developers, designers, social media managers who are working hard to provide perfect customer experience to our valuable consumers. They handle each order from pickup to delivery of the parcel.

We provide full customer support to our valuable consumers 24\*7. We have an inbuilt support system in our app. You can text or directly call our customer executives.
